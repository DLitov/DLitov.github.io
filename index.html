About Me

Hi, I’m Dimitar Litov, a passionate and detail-oriented Configuration Expert with a talent in turning ideas into impactful, pharma business-focused SaaS Applications.
I signed for DevOps Essentials course with Pragmatic, because I wanted to further develop my skills and knowledge in the Information Technology industry, and become fully aware of how the Software Development and DevOps lifecycle works.
During my DevOps Essentials course journey I gained essential knowledge into the following areas: Linux, Bash, Python, Networking, Web Servers and System Design, Virtualization and Containerization, Version Control Systems (Git), CI/CD Pipelines, Configuration Management (Ansible), DevSecOps.
The theoretical and practical skills that I gathered throughout the course will lay the groundwork for my future DevOps professional journey.
As a next step, I am planning to further delve into the realm of Python, because mastering this programming language can help you become part of the evolving AI and Machine Learning technologies. In addition, I am also planning to deep dive into the Containerization world by advancing my comprehension of Docker and Kubernetes.
